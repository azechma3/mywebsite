What is Twinkling Watermelon all about?

This drama explores the problem that many young people face: whether to pursue a life that makes them happy by doing what they enjoy, or to follow their parents' ambitions and meet their parents' expectations. Eun Gyeol, the main character who is a CODA (Child Of Deaf Adults), struggles to balance his passions and his family's demands until he is given the opportunity to change the direction of their lives by traveling back in time to when his parents were his age. We are also faced with the question: "Would our lives be better if we could change the past?".
