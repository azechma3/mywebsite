PLOT

Twinkling Watermelon Plot

Ha Eun Gyeol is a music lover, but he is also the trophy son of his deaf parents and the only one in his family who can connect with the outside world. He has a deaf brother who does taekwondo. So he needs to be responsible for all of them, but his guitar continues calling to him, and he's not sure if he should pursue music. One night, a miraculous event happens, and he travels in 1995, when his parents are now his age, and he is now going to play cupid for his future parents as the younger version of his father a vivacious kid who is in a band, chasing a popular girl, and has no interest in education.
