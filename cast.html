Casts of Twinkling Watermelon

Ryeoun as Eun-gyeol

One of the main characters and the son of Ha Yi-chan and Cheong-ah; also has a brother named Eun-ho.

Choi Hyun-Wook as young Ha Yi-chan

One of the main characters and is from the past(1995).

Shin Eun-soo as Cheong-ah

One of the main characters and is also from the past. A cold person who is born deaf.

Seol In-ah as young Se-kyung and Eun Yoo

Se-kyung is Eun Yoo's mother and Eun Yoo is 18 years old who looks just like her mother when she was at that age.

Bong Jae Hyun as Eun-ho

A jolly person who is born deaf. Eun Gyeol's older brother.

Choi Won-young as old Ha Yi-chan

Eun-gyeol and Eun-ho's father who is also deaf.

Jung Sang-hoon as master

A mysterious man who owns a music shop named La Vida. He is the one who let the two(Eun-gyeol & Eun-yoo) time travel.

Cheon Ho-Jin as Grandpa

Owner of Viva Music and he is the one who taught Eun-gyeol how to play the guitar.

Go Doo-shim as Go Yang-hee

Yi-chan's grandma who is feisty.

Seo Young-hee as old Cheong-ah

Eun-gyeol and Eun-ho's mother who is born deaf.
